London Locales & Iconic Destinations

1. Westminster

The political heart of London. Home to the Houses of Parliament, Big Ben, and the historic Westminster Abbey.

2. Covent Garden

A vibrant hub in the West End famous for its historic market, street performers, open-air cafes, and world-class theatres.

3. South Bank

Stretching along the River Thames, this cultural district features the London Eye, Tate Modern, and the National Theatre.
